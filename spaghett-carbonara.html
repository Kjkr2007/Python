Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with Parmesan cheese.
  4. Combine the spaghetti with pancetta, then stir in the egg mixture off the heat.
  5. Season with salt and pepper and serve immediately.
